Have you checked your smoke detectors lately?

Test units monthly. Install new batteries immediately when the low-battery warning alarm sounds or at least once a year. Clean smoke alarms regularly by vacuuming them with a brush attachment. Replace smoke alarms every 10 years to achieve optimum performance.
